About Nyumbani Haven

Nyumbani Haven represents a new standard in home-based healthcare delivery. We bridge the gap between institutional medical care and the comfort of home by providing clinical-grade services with personalized attention.

Our multidisciplinary team of licensed healthcare professionals is committed to delivering evidence-based care while maintaining the dignity, comfort, and individual preferences of each patient.

Founder & Clinical Director: Leah Nyambura Ngugi

With extensive clinical experience across hospital settings and community health programs, Leah established Nyumbani Haven to address the growing need for professional home-based healthcare in Kenya. Her vision combines clinical excellence with compassionate care delivery.

Our Professional Healthcare Team

All team members are licensed, certified, and undergo continuous professional development to ensure the highest standards of care

Registered Nurses

Licensed RNs with specialized training in home healthcare, medication management, wound care, and chronic disease management. All nurses maintain current certifications and participate in ongoing education.

Licensed Physiotherapists

Certified physical therapists specializing in home-based rehabilitation, mobility training, and therapeutic exercise programs. Expert in adaptive equipment and home safety assessments.

Clinical Psychologists

Licensed mental health professionals providing individual therapy, family counseling, grief support, and behavioral interventions. Specialized in medical psychology and chronic illness adaptation.

Specialized Care Coordinators

Certified healthcare coordinators managing complex care plans, family communication, and interdisciplinary collaboration to ensure seamless care delivery and optimal outcomes.
